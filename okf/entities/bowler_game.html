Table: bowler_game

Unique on (bowler_id, game_id).

ColumnTypeDescription
idBIGINT (PK)Identity.
bowler_idBIGINT (FK, not null)The Bowler.
game_idBIGINT (FK, not null)The Game.
total_scoreINTSum of frame scores; recomputed whenever frames are saved.
resultSTRING enumWIN, LOSS, or DRAW; set after all bowlers' frames are in.
game_pointsDOUBLESum of this bowler's frame points (spares/strikes) plus a 3-point bonus if they won the game (split evenly among ties).

Relationships

  • Many-to-one with Bowler and Game.
  • One-to-many with Frame.

Computed by

ScoreService.saveFrames / recalculateResults.
